ABOUT US

Conveniently located in the downtown area of Mojokerto city, LYNN Hotel Mojokerto aims to be the ideal "home away from home" for travelers who seeks for the essentials, ease and ultimate comfort in a travel while exploring the city's many destinations. Our guest rooms and facilities are modernly designed with inspiration from Mojokerto culture, creating an unique Mojokerto experience and soothing ambience for a restful stay. Buffet breakfast and a la carte menu are served by Astabrata restaurant, offering delicious range of Indonesian, Asian and Western dishes. Business guests and travelers can also benefit from our meeting facilities featured with modern audiovisual equipment.
